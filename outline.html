The Challenge

Outline Ventures came to us with a diverse inventory ranging from 3D signage fabrication to high-end corporate gifts and CCTV security systems. Their previous digital presence was fragmented, making it difficult for clients to understand the full scope of "You Name It, We Brand It."

They needed a scalable e-commerce platform that could handle complex product variations (like banner sizes and camera specs) while maintaining a sleek, corporate aesthetic that builds trust with B2B clients.

Our Solution

At Klints Solutions, we leveraged WordPress and WooCommerce to build a unified catalog system. We structured the site to distinctly separate their key verticals:

  • Visual Branding: A portfolio-style section showcasing LED signage and banners.
  • Corporate Gifting: A shopable catalog for items like the "Exquisite Travel Set" and "Dell Citilife Backpacks."
  • Security Division: Technical product pages for TP-Link Tapo cameras with detailed specification tabs.

We also integrated secure local payment gateways (M-Pesa, Visa) and optimized the site for local SEO keywords like "Branding in Nairobi" and "CCTV Installation."
