What is Klints Rental Manager?

Klints Rental Manager is the definitive property management system in Kenya, designed to simplify every aspect of rental operations for landlords and agencies. As a comprehensive rental management system, it unites rent collection, lease administration, maintenance tracking, financial reporting, tenant communications, and document storage under one intuitive dashboard.

In Kenya’s dynamic real estate market, keeping track of rent payments, lease renewals, and maintenance tasks across multiple properties can become overwhelming. Our property management system automates M-PESA receipts, sends timely reminders, and issues digital acknowledgements instantly — improving cash flow and tenant experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Klints Rental Manager support M-PESA rent collection?

Yes. We support Paybill and Till with automatic matching and digital receipts.

Can I generate Rent Roll and Aged Receivables?

Absolutely. Reports include Rent Roll, Aged Receivables, P&L, Cash Flow, and more.
